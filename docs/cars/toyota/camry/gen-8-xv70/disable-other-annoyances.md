# Disable other annoyances
The more modern cars are, the more annoying they become 

## Mute the seatbelt chime
Toyota's seatbelt chime in this Camry has to be the worst in this industry. It's not a chime, it's a fucking
siren. Either Toyota Techstream (if you can get your hands on it) or the Carista app over any generic ELM327
OBD Bluetooth/Wi-Fi adapters can turn the buzzers off. Each buzzer for driver/front passenger/rear
passenger can be turned off separately

## Turn off Body Control with Torque Demand
The car by default cut torque when it thinks there's too much pitch and bounce. I don't think you know better
than I do on the roads I travel, and this feature causes throttle response to be wildly inconsistent. Put
"Body Control with Torque" into Google and there are many people having the same issue. Again, Techstream/Carista
can turn this off

## Bypass motion lockout
I can kinda understand CarPlay/Android Auto keyboard locked out, but no album art for music player in motion, WTF?
TBW, found something on Toyota forums but haven't been able to verify that the pinout of my Camry's Panasonic
head unit (for JBL sound system) is consistent with other markets

NAViKS and Beat-Sonic sell wiring harnesses to bypass lockout for around $200 if you want the path of least resistance
